SevaSaathi is a dedicated digital platform designed to bridge the gap between senior citizens and government services in India. Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is SevaSaathi a government website? ‚ñº
No, SevaSaathi is not a government website. We are an independent guidance platform that helps you understand and access official government services. We redirect you to official government portals for all applications and submissions. Think of us as your helpful guide, not a replacement for government websites.
2. Is SevaSaathi free to use? ‚ñº
Yes, SevaSaathi is completely free to use. We do not charge any fees for our guidance services, tutorials, or support. All our resources and information are provided at no cost to help senior citizens access government schemes without any financial burden.
3. Do you collect or store my personal information? ‚ñº
No, we do not collect, store, or share any personal information. SevaSaathi only provides guidance and redirects you to official government portals where you can safely submit your information directly to the government. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.
4. How can I identify fake government messages? ‚ñº
Government agencies never ask for money via SMS or WhatsApp, never request OTPs or passwords, and always use official domains ending in .gov.in. Be cautious of messages with urgent language, spelling errors, or requests for immediate payment. Our Notice Explainer feature provides detailed guidance on identifying scams.
5. What government schemes are available for senior citizens? ‚ñº
There are many schemes including Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme, Pradhan Mantri Vaya Vandana Yojana, Senior Citizen Savings Scheme, health insurance under Ayushman Bharat, and various state-specific benefits. Visit our Government Schemes section for a complete list with eligibility criteria and application procedures.
6. Can I get help in my native language? ‚ñº
Yes! SevaSaathi provides guidance in Kannada and other regional languages. Use the language selector in the top menu to switch to your preferred language. We believe that language should never be a barrier to accessing government services and your rights.
7. How do I apply for a government scheme? ‚ñº
First, select the scheme you're interested in from our Government Schemes section. We'll provide step-by-step instructions, list the required documents, and explain eligibility criteria. When you're ready, we'll redirect you to the official government portal where you can complete your application safely and securely.
8. Where can I find help centers near me? ‚ñº
Use our Help Center Locator feature in the main menu. Enter your location (city or pin code), and we'll show you the nearest government offices, Jan Seva Kendras, Common Service Centers, and authorized help centers. You'll get addresses, contact numbers, and working hours for each location.
9. What documents do I need for most government schemes? ‚ñº
Common documents include Aadhaar card, PAN card, age proof (birth certificate or school certificate), address proof (electricity bill, ration card), bank account details with IFSC code, and recent passport-size photographs. Specific schemes may require additional documents, which we list in detail for each scheme in our guidance section.
10. How will I receive updates about new schemes? ‚ñº
After creating an account and logging in, you can enable phone notifications to receive alerts about new government schemes, application deadlines, and important updates. You can customize which types of notifications you want to receive, and you can turn them off anytime in your settings.
